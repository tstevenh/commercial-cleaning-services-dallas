## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Brand Voice Attributes
- **Professional but approachable:** Not stuffy, but credible
- **Local and knowledgeable:** Dallas area expertise shows
- **Direct and honest:** No fluff, no overpromising
- **Service-oriented:** Focus on client needs

### Writing Rules
1. **Answer the question first** - Don't bury the lead
2. **Be specific** - "Most offices" â†’ "90% of Dallas offices we service"
3. **Show don't tell** - Real examples over claims
4. **Use plain English** - No jargon without explanation
5. **Vary sentence length** - Short. Medium. And longer, more complex sentences for variety and rhythm.
6. **Admit limitations** - "We don't handle X, but here's who we recommend"

### Words/Phrases to Avoid (AI Tells)
- delve, dive into, dig into
- comprehensive, robust, cutting-edge
- utilize, leverage (as a verb)
- crucial, vital, essential (overused)
- landscape, navigate, streamline
- tapestry, multifaceted, myriad
- "In today's world"
- "It's important to note"
- "Let's dive in"
- "In conclusion"
